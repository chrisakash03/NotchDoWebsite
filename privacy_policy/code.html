Privacy Policy

NotchDo is built with privacy at its core. We don't collect, store, or sell your data — ever.

block

No data collection

NotchDo does not collect any personal information, usage analytics, crash reports, or telemetry. There are no third-party trackers, advertising SDKs, or hidden data pipelines in the app.

laptop_mac

Your data stays on your Mac

All of your tasks and settings are stored locally on your device. NotchDo has no servers and never receives your content. Your information never leaves your Mac unless you explicitly choose to sync via iCloud.

cloud

iCloud sync (optional)

If you enable iCloud sync, your data is transmitted and stored through Apple's iCloud service — governed by Apple's privacy policy, not ours. NotchDo does not have access to your iCloud credentials or synced data on any external server we operate.
